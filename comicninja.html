Calling All Superstar Writers!

If you’re a superstar writer with a mind that can weave exciting stories, we need you! Not only do you win awesome cash rewards, but we are also giving you a golden opportunity to be one of the writers of our debut comic book.

How to Qualify:

  • You must be following IAD on Instagram, Facebook, Twitter, and TikTok to follow the next set of instructions.
  • Send a one-page introduction of yourself and a one-page sample story written by you to iadstudioslagos@gmail.com before the 10th of March 2025.
  • Make sure you capture your interests, the type of stories you like to write, and your goals.
  • Shortlisted candidates will be contacted and given the necessary briefing to develop the next two chapters in our soon-to-be-released comic book project.
  • The best entries will be selected, and 5 winners will be chosen.

What’s in it for you?

  • Cash prizes of N50,000 each.
  • An opportunity to intern with IAD for 6 months to work on other projects.
  • Exclusive access to the IAD release party.
  • The exclusive opportunity to join the team of writers for the comic book rollout.

Why Participate?

    At IAD, we stand for creativity and innovation. We cultivate open-mindedness and curiosity towards life. We believe in diverse ways to connect with our audience and consumers.
    We want to build a sustainable community through multiple platforms - music, events, arts, fashion, and physical expressions, all embedded with love for the planet.

Our Core Values:

  • Collaboration
  • Resilience
  • Innovation
  • Fun-loving
  • Empathy
  • Creativity

Remember to send your entries to iadstudioslagos@gmail.com before the 10th of March 2025. Don’t miss out on this!
